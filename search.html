    APPLE BUTTER

    London

    Apple Butter Café offers a warm welcome for all with its all-day dining menu, cosy yet sophisticated surroundings in amongst an iconic and ultimately instagrammable apple tree, planted centre stage for all to see and enjoy. The concept has been created by successful Middle Eastern-born business operators and brothers Saleh and Mohammad Alayan alongside Faycal Abdel Khalek, each delivering something special and unique to its ultimate creation. Collectively bringing years of experience, passion and success with all things hospitality, the trio chose the heart of London to launch their debut east meets west fusion café concept.

    Xuecong

    CROSSTOWN DOUGHNUTS

    London

    Crosstown Doughnuts are dotted across London, from Victoria to Spitalfields. With a wide selection of sweet treats, from doughnuts to cookies, and with great options for vegans. Crosstown rolls all their own dough by hand, with all the fillings, jams, and glazes also made by them. You'll also find delicious cookies with flavours including: peanut butter and chocolate chip, oat and apricot, and chocolate chip.

    PIN SOCIAL CLUB

    Liverpool

    PINS Social Club is the place you need to be if you want to have fun. They aren’t just a place to go for a drink, even if their drinks are incredible, they’re a venue for a full evening event. Drinks they do include Blood Orangecello, The Little Lebowski, If you fancy something to eat they do Sunday Roasts, Brunch, Pizzas, Burgers and so much more. They also have a rooftop area with live music, perfect for when it gets sunny in the summer months.

    THE BAR WITH SHAPES FOR A NAME

    London

    A hidden gem on Kingsland Road. The name itself is not actually a name, rather a description of its label which is three Bauhaus-influenced primary-coloured shapes: a yellow triangle, a red square and a blue circle (but people call it Shapes for short). It has an almost classroom or educational feel to it and there are no bottles on show, making it difficult to know the venue is a bar if you didn't already know. Excellent cocktails in an unusual setting and decent value for money, which makes it a must visit.

    Jess
