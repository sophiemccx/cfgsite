Eggham on Toast

Egham Hill, Egham TW20 0BQ

★★★★★

This little gem is located in the heart of Egham, it's a great place to visit with excellent friendly staff. With a wide range of delicious dishes from English/American style breakfasts, this cafe was one of the first cafe of its kind it Egham. Its great place to brunch with friends, have a discreet business breakfast or lunch meeting or just to come and relax and read the paper.
