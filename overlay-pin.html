Pins Social Club

45 - 61 Duke St, Liverpool L1 5AP

★★★★★

PINS Social Club is the place you need to be if you want to have fun. They aren’t just a place to go for a drink, even if their drinks are incredible, they’re a venue for a full evening event.

Drinks they do include Blood Orangecello, The Little Lebowski, If you fancy something to eat they do Sunday Roasts, Brunch, Pizzas, Burgers and so much more. Happy Hour is Monday to Fridays, 5-7pm, perfect for an after work catch up.
